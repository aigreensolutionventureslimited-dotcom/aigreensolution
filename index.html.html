About Us

Africa's Trusted Agricultural Export Partner

Founded in 2012, A.I Green Solutions has grown from a regional supplier to a fully integrated international exporter of premium agricultural commodities. Headquartered in Nigeria with sourcing networks across West and East Africa, we serve buyers in Europe, Asia, the Americas, and the Middle East.

Our vertically integrated model — from farm partnerships to port handling — ensures unmatched consistency, traceability, and quality at every step. We work exclusively with verified smallholder cooperatives and certified commercial farms that meet our exacting standards.

Quality Assurance

From Farm to Port — Complete Traceability

Our end-to-end supply chain management ensures every shipment meets the quality and compliance standards expected by buyers worldwide.

01
🌱

Farmer Sourcing

We partner with 2,000+ verified smallholder cooperatives and certified farms across Nigeria. All partners undergo annual audits and must meet our sourcing criteria on pesticide use, harvesting practices, and post-harvest handling.

02
🔬

Lab Testing

Every incoming batch is tested at our in-house lab and by SGS-accredited third-party labs for moisture content, aflatoxin, pesticide residues, and microbial contamination before acceptance.

03
⚙️

Processing

Commodities are cleaned, sorted, dried, and graded in our ISO 22000-certified facility using modern equipment including optical sorters, fluidized bed dryers, and industrial cleaning lines.

04
📦

Packaging

Food-grade packaging materials meeting destination-country requirements. Options include jute bags, polypropylene sacks, vacuum-packed tins, IBC drums, and bulk containers with moisture barriers.

05
📋

Export Docs

We prepare all export documentation including Certificate of Origin, Phytosanitary Certificate, SGS Pre-Shipment Inspection Certificate, Bills of Lading, Fumigation Certificate, and commercial invoice/packing list.

06
🚢

Port & Delivery

Containers are stuffed under supervision and sealed before port departure. We work with Apapa, Tin Can, and Onne ports with regular sailings to Europe, Asia, and the Americas.

Shipping & Logistics

Reliable Global Delivery

We manage the complete export logistics process — from container booking and stuffing to customs clearance and delivery confirmation at destination.

Shipping Options

🚢

FCL — Full Container Load

20ft and 40ft containers. Ideal for large orders. Direct stuffing at our facility for maximum control.

Transit: 14–35 days depending on destination
📦

LCL — Less than Container Load

Consolidated cargo for smaller orders. We handle groupage at Apapa and Tin Can ports.

Transit: 21–45 days (includes consolidation)
✈️

Air Freight

For urgent, high-value, or small-volume shipments. Available via Lagos, Abuja, and Kano airports.

Transit: 3–7 days worldwide
